About Our School

Empowering Students Since Establishment

Shree Janata Secondary School, located in Ramdhuni-3, Singya, Sunsari, has been a cornerstone of quality education in the region. We are committed to nurturing young minds through a balanced approach of academic excellence and character development.

Our Civil Engineering program, introduced in 2076, is a pioneering initiative in the region, providing hands-on technical education starting from Grade 9 with limited seats to ensure personalized attention.
